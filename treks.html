Trekking in Nepal

World-class trails for every level — from beginner to seasoned mountaineer

Everest Base Camp Trek

The iconic journey to the foot of the world's tallest mountain, passing through Sherpa villages, monasteries, and dramatic glacial terrain.

Hard 14 Days 5,364 m

Annapurna Circuit

A classic circumnavigation of the Annapurna massif crossing the legendary Thorong La Pass with stunning views and diverse landscapes.

Moderate 18 Days 5,416 m

Poon Hill Trek

A short and rewarding trek in the Annapurna foothills, famous for its golden sunrise over the Dhaulagiri and Annapurna ranges.

Easy 5 Days 3,210 m

Langtang Valley Trek

A lesser-trodden trail through rhododendron forests and Tamang villages leading to the stunning Langtang glacier.

Moderate 10 Days 3,870 m

Upper Mustang Trek

A remote trek through the ancient walled city of Lo Manthang in a restricted, arid region that feels like stepping into another era.

Moderate 12 Days 3,840 m

Ghorepani Poon Hill

Ideal for first-time trekkers, this trail winds through charming Gurung and Magar villages with incredible panoramic mountain views.

Easy 4 Days 3,210 m
